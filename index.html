This website presents fieldwork and publication materials from Mark Slobin's research in Afghanistan. There are three layers of organization, with increasing access to the technical materials of the study:
  1. an introduction to the ideas and topics with capsule summaries;
  2. links to excerpts from the book-length study;
  3. the entire book itself, Music in the Culture of Northern Afghanistan (1976)

Still photography, moving picture clips, and sound clips accompany the written text.
